# Reviews

Honest reviews of endodontic courses and equipment, written by the people who took them or bought them.

**Want to add a review?** Click [**Edit in browser**](/admin/), sign in with GitHub, pick *Course reviews* or *Equipment reviews*, click *New*. The form walks you through every field. No markdown, no git.

Or see the [review-writing guide](write-review.md) for the full markdown template.

## Browse

- [Course reviews](reviews/courses/) — masterclasses, online CE, residency offerings, hands-on workshops
- [Equipment reviews](reviews/equipment/) — apex locators, endomotors, microscopes, file systems, irrigation tools

## What makes a review useful

Everyone reads reviews before spending money. The reviews that actually help say:

- **What you specifically were using / doing before**
- **What the product or course does that its marketing doesn't mention**
- **What it doesn't do well**
- **Who it's a good fit for and who shouldn't bother**
- **Price relative to the alternatives, not just the absolute price**

If your review has none of that, it's not very useful. See the [editorial guidelines](editorial-guidelines.md).
